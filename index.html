Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from enterprise procurement tools?

Enterprise tools cost $50K-500K+ and take 6-12 months to implement. They're designed for Fortune 500 companies with complex procurement needs. VendorFlow is built specifically for mid-market companies (20-200 employees) who just need a simple way to onboard vendors without email chains. Setup takes 5 minutes, not 6 months.

Can I customize the vendor onboarding form?

Yes! You can add custom fields for industry-specific requirements (e.g., safety certifications for construction, food safety certs for restaurants, etc.). The core fields (W-9, bank info, insurance) come pre-configured with validation.

Does it integrate with my accounting system?

VendorFlow exports to CSV format that works with QuickBooks, NetSuite, Xero, and any accounting system that accepts CSV imports. Direct integrations with QuickBooks and NetSuite are coming in Q2 2026.

What if my vendor has trouble filling out the form?

The vendor form is mobile-friendly and includes help text for every field. If they get stuck, they can email our support team or contact your procurement person directly through the platform. We also provide a "Save and Continue Later" feature so vendors can complete it in multiple sessions.

Is my vendor data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest (AES-256). We're SOC 2 Type II compliant and follow industry best practices for data security. Your vendor data is never shared with third parties.

Can I try it before paying?

Absolutely! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required. If you're not seeing time savings within the first week, we'll extend your trial for another 14 days.
